comparison operator <=>
value1 <=> value2

----------------------------------------------
|   -1  |   Less than   |   Moves "left"     |
----------------------------------------------
|   0   |   Equal       |   Stays            |
----------------------------------------------
|   1   |   More than   |   Moves "right"    |
----------------------------------------------

array = [5,8,2,6,1,3]
x = array.sort{|v1,v2| v1<=>v2} # Returns [1, 2, 3, 5, 6, 8]
x = array.sort{|v1,v2| v2<=>v1} # Returns [8, 6, 5, 3, 2, 1]
